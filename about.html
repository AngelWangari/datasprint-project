About Data Sprint

DataSprint is an open platform built to accelerate data science learning for young people across Kenya and Africa.

Our Mission 🎯

Empowering the next generation of African data scientists by providing simplified learning resources, mentorship, and collaborative projects.

Our Vision 👁‍🗨

To create a vibrant community of young African minds using data to solve real-world problems.
